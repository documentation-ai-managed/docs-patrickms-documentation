## Account Setup

To begin using Nova Gestion, first create an account through our website at novagestion.eu. The registration process is straightforward and requires basic information about your business.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Website" icon="globe">
    Navigate to novagestion.eu and click the registration button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Details" icon="user">
    Enter your business information, contact details, and preferred domain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your email for a verification link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Login" icon="log-in">
    Access your dashboard with your newly created credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Domain Configuration

Once your account is set up, you'll need to configure your domain settings. Our platform supports various domain providers and offers easy integration.

<Callout kind="info">Ensure your domain is pointed to our nameservers before proceeding with website setup.</Callout>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="New Domain" icon="plus">
    Register a new domain through our partners or use an existing one.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Existing Domain" icon="refresh-cw">
    Update your DNS settings to point to our hosting servers.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Website Creation

Our dashboard provides an intuitive interface for creating and managing websites. Choose from various templates or upload your own content.

<CodeGroup tabs="HTML,CSS">
  ```html
  <!DOCTYPE html>
  <html lang="en">
  <head>
    <meta charset="UTF-8">
    <title>Your Website</title>
    <link rel="stylesheet" href="styles.css">
  </head>
  <body>
    <h1>Welcome to Nova Gestion</h1>
    <p>Your online presence starts here.</p>
  </body>
  </html>
  ```
  ```css
  body {
    font-family: Arial, sans-serif;
    margin: 0;
    padding: 20px;
  }
  h1 {
    color: #3B82F6;
  }
  ```
</CodeGroup>
